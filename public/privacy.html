Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

Our phishing prevention system collects the following information:

  • Reported email metadata (sender, subject, timestamp)
  • User account information (username and encrypted password)
  • System logs of security events
  • Training completion records

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information exclusively for:

  • Identifying and analyzing phishing attempts
  • Improving our security systems
  • Providing security training
  • Generating security reports

No personal email content is stored beyond what is necessary for security analysis.

3. Data Retention

We retain different types of information for varying periods:

  • Reported emails: 6 months
  • Security logs: 12 months
  • User accounts: Until deletion request
  • Training records: 24 months

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact your organization's security administrator.

5. Security Measures

We implement robust security measures including:

  • Encryption of all stored data
  • Role-based access controls
  • Regular security audits
  • Anonymization of reports

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
